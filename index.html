How we met

Our love story began at the NYC Library through mutual friends. From the moment we started talking, we just clicked in a way that felt natural and effortless. We quickly discovered our shared passion for hiking and the outdoors, which became the foundation of many adventures together. Our relationship deepened as we explored trails, mountains, and nature's beauty side by side. The proposal was a dream come true - Laxman proposed in the breathtaking Upper Peninsula, surrounded by the wilderness we both love so much. It was the perfect setting for the beginning of our next chapter together.
